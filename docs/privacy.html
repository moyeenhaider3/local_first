Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Local First ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use the Local First app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Numbers: We use Firebase Authentication to log you in securely via your phone number.
  • Location Data: We require approximate and precise location data to help you find nearby rental items and service workers.
  • Personal Information (KYC): If you choose to undergo identity verification, we collect and securely store your KYC documents.
  • Transaction History: We store the history of your agreements and milestone verifications in Firestore.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Local First service.
  • To build your Trust Score based on completed agreements.
  • To verify your identity for the safety of the community.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment Information: Local First does not process payments or collect credit card information. All transactions happen externally (e.g., via UPI).
  • In-App Chat: We do not host in-app chat. Communication is handled externally via platforms like WhatsApp.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with standard analytics or crash reporting services if enabled.

5. Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time from within the app settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team.
